Join our team

Founded by classmates Thoma, Alban, Ermina, Petraq, Sanja and Aulona in 2022, a travel mix-up sparked a revolutionary approach to tourism. We were part of a dynamic group and we are were passionate about travelling and discovering out different areas of our beloved, undiscovered country. The result? A platform that gathers the best activities across Albania, all in one place.

From humble beginnings in a small student room to becoming the industry leader, a lot has changed at GuideAlbania. What hasn’t is our commitment to creating amazing experiences and ensuring that you love where you’re going.
